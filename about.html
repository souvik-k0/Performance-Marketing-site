The Origin Story

Scaling a D2C brand in today's crowded market is incredibly painful. In 2016, our founders noticed a major gap: agencies were charging massive retainers to manually manage simple catalog spreadsheets.

We set out to turn those manual spreadsheets into a living, breathing software brain — algorithms that automatically ingest massive product feeds, assign budgets locally, and kill underperforming ads silently in the night.

Today, BrandBiography forms the backbone of digital revenue for over 350 D2C, B2B, and Enterprise marketplace brands across 15 countries.
